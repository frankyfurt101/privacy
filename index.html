DreamWhisper respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information DreamWhisper collects, how it is used, how it may be shared, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide, such as your name, email address, and profile details.
  • Dream-related content you create in the app, including dream titles, transcriptions, recordings, reflections, comments, and other content you choose to store or share.
  • Social information related to your activity in the app, including friend connections, likes, comments, notifications, and shared dream activity.
  • Device and app usage information, such as device type, operating system version, app version, analytics events, crash or error logs, and general diagnostic information.
  • Push notification tokens and related notification preferences.
  • Cloud synchronization metadata needed to support sync and storage features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve DreamWhisper.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Store, sync, and display your dream content.
  • Support social features such as sharing, likes, comments, notifications, and friend connections.
  • Process dream-related content to generate interpretations, summaries, themes, emotions, prompts, and related app features.
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve reliability.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. How Information May Be Shared

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, such as cloud infrastructure, analytics, storage, notification, AI/processing, and related technical service providers.
  • When you choose to share content with other users through public, friends-only, or other visibility settings.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to applicable legal requirements.

4. Your Choices

You may be able to access and update parts of your profile information within the app, control visibility settings for dream content, export or delete your account data where such functionality is provided, and manage notifications through app settings or device settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain legitimate business records. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and how it is used. When you delete your account, we may delete or anonymize personal information associated with your account, while retaining de-identified, aggregated, or legally required records where permitted.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information, but no method of transmission, cloud storage, or electronic processing is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed. In the event of a suspected security incident, we may investigate, contain, remediate, and provide notices as required by applicable law.

7. Children’s Privacy

DreamWhisper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate legal authorization. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

8. International Use

If you use DreamWhisper from outside the country where its systems are operated, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions, which may have different data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we may revise the effective date and provide additional notice where appropriate. Continued use of the app after an update means the updated policy applies to your use of the service.
